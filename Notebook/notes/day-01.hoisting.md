## What the hell is Hoisting? 

Happens when an interpreter moves the declaration of functions, scope, classes and blocks of code to the top of their sco
pe.

Informal ways of describing what hoisting is:

1. Being able to use a variable before the line it's been declared.
2. And Functions and Declarations the same.


**Note:** Var has no Block Scope

When thinking of an example for hoisting, just take Var. It can be used before it's been declared.
